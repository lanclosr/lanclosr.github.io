Exploring Cancer Rates
About this App
Explore any relationship that might exist between nitrate well samples and the rate of cancer within census tracts of Wisconsin.

Enter a cell size to the left, in kilometers, to interpolate the well sample data. This data is used to conduct a linear regression and visually show where there is under (blue) or over (red) predicition of the cancer rates based on the analysis.

Values below 5km will take time to process. Please be patient!
